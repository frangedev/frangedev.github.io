"In a world where everyone tries to be something, be nothing. Let your destination be nothingness.

FRANGE is an audio-visual project blending deep electronic beats with immersive visual experiences. Based in Istanbul, FRANGE explores the boundaries between live performance, DJing, and VJing using custom setups and reactive visuals.

From "Intimate" to "Aura", and now the latest single "Electric Whispers", the journey is about creating atmospheres that resonate with the inner void and the electric soul.
